Our Flooring Services

From restoration to transformation.

Some floors need a light refresh. Some need structural repair or a full reset. We look at wear, moisture, movement, finish condition, and how the room is used before recommending the path forward.

Dustless Refinishing

Sanding and containment practices reduce airborne dust while preparing the floor for a smooth, durable finish.

Hardwood Installation

Solid or engineered hardwood installed with attention to subfloor prep, layout, transitions, and long-term stability.

Screen & Recoat

For sound floors with surface wear, a screen and recoat refreshes protection without sanding to bare wood.

Water Damage

Moisture concerns, warped boards, and damaged sections are evaluated before repair or replacement recommendations.

Tile & Grout

Professional tile and grout cleaning targets embedded soil in porous grout, with sealing options to help resist future stains.

Stair & Trim

Stair treads, risers, trim, and transitions finished to make the whole room feel complete.

Choosing The Right Service

The right fix depends on the floor.

Recoat

For floors that are dull but structurally sound, a screen and recoat can add a fresh protective layer without sanding to bare wood.

Refinish

For deeper scratches, dark stains, peeling finish, or a color change, sanding and refinishing gives the floor a true reset.

Repair Or Replace

For warped boards, water damage, loose areas, or unstable sections, repair or new installation may be the cleaner long-term choice.
