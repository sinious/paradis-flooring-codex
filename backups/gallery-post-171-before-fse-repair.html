What To Look For

Details matter more than shine alone.

A strong floor should look settled in the room, not overworked. Look at edge detail, consistency, transitions, and how naturally the sheen sits in the light.

Finish Quality

Look for even sheen, clean edges, and a finish that feels natural to the room instead of heavy or cloudy.

Repair Detail

Good repair work blends into the floor with tight transitions, stable boards, and no obvious interruption in the grain.

Estimate Notes

If a project looks close to your floor condition, mention it when you reach out so we can compare the right service path.
